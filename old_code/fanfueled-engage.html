FanFueled Engage

FanFueled is an engagement marketing company that builds social gaming solutions for brands, artists and festivals. We use patented software to track the ripple of influence, incentivizing peer to peer marketing.

Engage is a social gaming platform where users can engage in their daily social interactions with brands, artists and festivals. Users earn points for completing the social actions, so by accumulating a certain number of points users will be able to redeem them for prizes or rewards or offers from the brand or artist or festivals.

Fan fueled engage is built ground up with mobile in mind. Each engage platform should be able to accommodate all the social interactions such as Facebook likes, tweets and checkin's. Other actions such as watching YouTube videos, listening to songs, completing custom quiz, purchases and many other can be added to an engage platform.

Each platform can have up to 40 actions on average and 20 varieties or rewards. Some engage platforms can pull up to 30,000 users. There is a leaderboard for each engage displayed up to 12 places to show the competition for the prizes.
