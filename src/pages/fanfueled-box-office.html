FanFueled Box Office

Box office as you guessed already is an app for touch screens at event venues to sell tickets. Fan fueled hosts events and users can purchase tickets through web app. But some clients needed a service to sell tickets at the venue door.

With the need for such an application we created an web based touchscreen that can be accesses at a kiosk installed at the venue.

This app let's venue managers sell tickets at the door just by swiping a credit card. The app is developed to manage multiple personnel at kiosk and ability to purchase with cash as well. The receives are sent to customers email address and hard tickets are printed at the kiosk.
